#  Project Overview

The Style Niche Dashboard is a professional and interactive **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to provide comprehensive insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, regional performance, and product category analysis.

This dashboard transforms raw retail data into actionable business intelligence through KPI monitoring, interactive visualizations, and dynamic reporting.

The solution helps stakeholders track business performance, identify opportunities, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

---

#  Project Objectives

- Analyze retail sales performance.
- Monitor revenue, cost, and profitability.
- Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
- Compare performance across retail locations.
- Analyze product category contribution.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Support strategic business decision-making.

#  Dashboard Insights

The dashboard enables users to:

- Monitor overall business profitability.
- Compare performance across store locations.
- Analyze customer retention and acquisition.
- Evaluate product category contribution.
- Track monthly revenue and profit trends.
- Identify top-performing locations and segments.
- Support data-driven business decisions.

---

#  Regional Performance Analysis

The dashboard provides sales analysis across multiple retail locations:

- Boutique Lane
- City Mall
- Suburb Strip

This helps identify top-performing regions and optimize business operations.

---

#  Product Category Analysis

The dashboard analyzes performance across product categories:

- Accessories
- Casual Wear
- Footwear
- Formal Wear

Users can evaluate category contribution to revenue and profitability.

---

#  Customer Segmentation

Customer behavior is analyzed through:

- New Customers
- Returning Customers

This enables businesses to understand retention patterns and customer loyalty.
